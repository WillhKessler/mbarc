A call to all bikepackers, dirt-road tourers, and gravel cyclists in Eastern Massachusetts: unite! We might not have the endless gravel roads of Maine, the leg-breaking climbs of Vermont, or the vistas of New Hampshire, but hidden gems abound; you just need to know how to find them.
